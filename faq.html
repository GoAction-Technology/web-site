FREQUENTLY ASKED

GoAction is a move to earn project where you can earn tokens while cycling and socialize with community organized events.

Our mission is not only to revolutionize the move to earn industry by encouraging more people to improve their health and achieve their moving goals but also by shifting profits away from platform owners to platform users through a unique decentralized platform

Click Here -> White Paper

We use Binance Smart Chain because we want to minimize transaction fees for users, as well as its fast and secure infrastructure. We will announce our Multichain upgrade in the quarter indicated in the Roadmap.

Our mobile application consists of 2 main sections, GoBike and GoSocial. In GoBike, you will be able to follow your ride tracking, the coins you earn and statistics live. In GoSocial, after completing the registration process, we enable you to create a social environment with other cyclists with profile, event and group ride (1.5x earnings) sections.

With ECO, the utility token you earn while riding, you can repair your bike, buy extra energy and convert it into GBK, the governance token.

With the governance token GAT, you can cash out the utility token ECO you earn while cycling and contribute to the combustion mechanism. Thus we get a deflationary structure.

To be announced

GAT, which has a deflationary structure, is separating from other move to earn projects and is making its own revolution. We stand out as the token with the least and fixed supply in the move to earn category. We eliminate the problem of investor confidence with our mechanism that will permanently raise the price, except for the supply.

ECO is the first in-app deflationary project with unlimited supply. It is a token that is constantly exposed to combustion and allows users to convert it to GAT, the governance token, at a fixed parity and derives its value from GBK's infrastructure.

GAT is a token released with a fixed supply. It draws its infrastructure from Binance Smart Chain, the world's most powerful network. It is also a governance token that is investor-friendly and rewards users for cycling on our project.

We consulted numerous crypto economists on this issue and chose a fixed supply of 600,000,000,000. We thought that creating new ones could pose a trust problem for investors.

GoAction will be available on Google Play and App Store. Stay Tuned
