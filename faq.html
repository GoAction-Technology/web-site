GoAction, with its deflationary structure, is a social platform that overcomes the economic problems in the projects made in the Move to Earn category, that you can use and earn for free, and that will revolutionize the Move to Earn category, built with blockchain technology.

Our mission is not only to revolutionize the move to earn  industry by encouraging more people to improve their health and achieve their moving goals but also by shifting profits away from platform owners to platform users through a unique decentralized platform.

We use Binance Smart Chain because we want to minimize transaction fees for users, as well as its fast and secure infrastructure. We will announce our Multichain upgrade in the quarter indicated in the Roadmap.

Our mobile application consists of 2 main sections, GoAction and GoSocial. In GoAction, you will be able to follow your ride tracking, the coins you earn and statistics live. In GoSocial, after completing the registration process, we enable you to create a social environment with other cyclists with profile, event and group ride (1.5x earnings) sections.

With ECO, the utility token you earn while riding, you can repair your bike, buy extra energy and convert it into GAT, the governance token.

With the governance token GAT, you can cash out the utility token ECO you earn while cycling and contribute to the combustion mechanism. Thus we get a deflationary structure.
