FREQUENTLY ASKED

GoAction is a deflationary project based on the sharing economy, where users can earn tokens by cycling or walking. Additionally, advertisers can increase their earnings by purchasing digital advertising space on the backs of bicycles or backpacks

Goal
The goal of GoAction is to promote healthy living worldwide by incentivizing physical activity and creating a happier and healthier society. Additionally, it aims to provide advertisers with an effective digital marketing platform to increase brand awareness and facilitate access to their target audience.

Mission
GoAction's mission is to encourage users to adopt an active lifestyle and provide them with a rewarding experience in the process. Simultaneously, it seeks to assist advertisers in enhancing brand visibility by offering an innovative and impactful advertising platform. Operating on the principles of the sharing economy, GoAction fosters community engagement and works towards increasing the value for participants.

GoMove, NFT Marketplace, GoWallet, Leaderboard

Upgrading NFTs allows users to improve the characteristics of their NFTs. These benefits include increased earnings, improved stats such as stamina and health, and higher level rewards. You can upgrade your NFT by going to its detail page and clicking the upgrade button.

GoAction takes security measures to protect user privacy and keeps user data confidential. The platform uses encryption and security protocols to protect the personal information of its users.

GoAction can be downloaded from the Google Play Store, or the Apple Store

It can be downloaded from the Google Play Store and the App Store and logged in with an E-mail or Phone number optionally. In order to convert the earned ECO$s into cash, a hot wallet link must be established within the application. There is no need to purchase NFT or login with reference code to use the app

The tokens used on the GoAction platform are ECO and GAT. ECO tokens are rewards that users earn for physical activity, while GAT is the platform's governance token. ECO tokens are earned through activity, while GAT tokens can be bought on exchanges or earned in different ways within the platform.

On the GoAction platform, users can buy, sell or trade NFTs. NFT trading takes place through the NFT marketplace within the platform. Users can select and buy the NFT of their choice or put their own NFTs up for sale.
