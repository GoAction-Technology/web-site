Events created by GoAction have some businesses at the end point. Our users who participate in this activity and our users who successfully complete this activity can earn discounts from businesses with the code they earn special to the event.

The benefits of GoAction events are that they bring customers to businesses and discounts to users.

On the event detail page, you can view the location, start and end point, date, time, event creator and participants' profile. There is also a button to join and send a message. You can easily contact the event owner by clicking the message button.

Events that are close to users in terms of location are ranked first.

You can sort events by city, district and many other features.

GoDate is designed to enable users to have more meaningful interactions with each other. This module matches users based on their common interests, suggests optional random routes, and organizes users' meetings by providing discounts from businesses.

Unlike other apps, GoDate plans users' first date by suggesting random routes to users, directing them to a business at the end of these routes and providing discounts from that business.

You don't need to set a route to get discounts from businesses, users can earn discounts from businesses that partner with us in the district and date of their choice on a daily basis and plan their first dates with GoAction's support.

You can use it in a simple way by clicking on GoDate from the bottom bar and swiping right on the people you like. If there is a mutual match, a private message box will open and you will have access to their GoAction profile.

Before logging in to GoDate, users will be asked questions that can generally identify the people they need to mark. Users will be matched with people with whom the answers to these questions are close in percentage terms and with people in their immediate surroundings.

GoAction creates your GoDate profile with questions to be asked before you log in, your name and photos from your GoAction profile.

Only users' names appear in the GoDate profile. No user can access each other's profiles without being matched.

If you do an activity together on the created route, you will earn 1.5 times more than usual.

If you are creating a route, at the end of the activity, that is, when you finish the activity in front of the business we have determined, you will see a code specific to that business, date and time and you can get a discount with this code. If you are not creating a route, you can optionally view the businesses in the district you have selected on the page with the Create route option, click on one of these businesses to get information and get a discount code specific to the date you will go.  

It will only be in your inbox with the person you are mutually matched with.

If a route is to be created, both parties can send routes, but only one route can be selected. If no route is to be created, both parties need to click on the business they are going to and get a discount code.
