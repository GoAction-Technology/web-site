USEFUL

GoAction is a social platform that brings users' walking and cycling movements into a sustainable earning system and allows users to organize their own activities and socialize with other users.

Goal
Our vision is to bring the Game-Fi world together with Social-Fi without sacrificing users to the inflationary system. to unite and create a revolution.

Mission
Our mission is to create a sustainable ecosystem that enables users to participate in the Web3 world. By creating a social environment as well as exercise, we want to push people within the same community towards a more profitable system of action against climate change in the world.

By creating events on GoSocial, users can socialize with their close friends or people they haven't met yet and earn more than their normal earnings as a result. The purpose of GoSocial is to create a social network where users interact outside.

NOTE: Creating events and group activities with multiple people will earn 1.5 times more than your normal earnings

It can be downloaded from the Google Play Store and the App Store and logged in with an E-mail or Phone number optionally. In order to convert the earned ECO$s into cash, a hot wallet link must be established within the application. There is no need to purchase NFT or login with reference code to use the app.

Creating new supplies could pose a trust problem for investors. Therefore we consulted with numerous crypto economists on this issue and chose a fixed supply of 6.000.000.000
